he/him is a pretty close, and very convenient, approximation to how I feel about myself, gender-wise.

I am fine with being referred to by any pronouns. If you ask me what to call me, I will always say he/him.

Nouns I am pickier about. I prefer neutral or masculine nouns, except the word man. A man is a lot of things that I am not and do not wish to be.

Men, plural, is a common and practical term for a group of male-presenting individuals. In this sense, I am okay with being included in a group of men; I simply would rather not be singled out as a man.
